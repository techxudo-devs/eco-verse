SECTION 1: HERO SECTION
File: components/home/Hero.tsx + Cards.tsx
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

You are a conversion copywriter for EchoVerse 360, a tech-led influencer
marketing and creator economy platform in Pakistan. Write the HERO section
copy for their homepage.

Context: The audience is Pakistani brand marketers, CMOs, and business owners
(25-45 yrs) who are tired of paying influencer agencies that deliver followers
but no sales. They've been burned before. They're skeptical but hungry for
real growth.

Tone: Confident. Sharp. Like a friend who's worked at WPP globally and is
now building something transformational right here in Pakistan. Not arrogant
- visionary.

Write:
1. A pre-headline label (5-7 words max) - the type above the main heading.
   Something like "Pakistan's Creator Economy, Redefined"
2. A main headline (4-8 words, ALL CAPS style, bold statement). This should
   feel like a punch - not a tagline. It should make a CMO stop scrolling.
3. A sub-headline (1 sentence, 15-25 words). Should speak to the shift from
   social media activity to measurable business outcomes.

Journey moment this section must create: "I've finally found someone who
speaks my language - not engagement rates, but actual ROI."

Do NOT use: "innovative", "one-stop-shop", "360 solution", "we are the best",
generic agency clichés.

File: components/home/Expertise.jsx

You are writing for EchoVerse 360's Expertise section. This section has 3
accordion items. Pakistani brands are the audience - from FMCG to retail to
D2C startups.

The section currently has a headline: "REASONING TO BETTER: RESONATING."
Keep the headline but write everything below it.

For each of these 3 services, write:
- A pill/tag list (5-8 short tags, 2-4 words each) that show DEPTH of
  capability - not just what the service is, but what they actually DO
  inside it. Make these feel like deliverables a Pakistani CMO would
  recognize and want.
- An optional 1-line description that could appear as a tooltip or
  expanded state (20-30 words) that speaks to the outcome, not the service.

Services to write for:
A) SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY - For Pakistani brands trying to cut through noise
   on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
B) CONTENT CREATION - Creator-led, platform-native, shot for scroll-stopping
   performance
C) COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT - Not just replies, but audience intelligence and
   brand trust building

Journey moment: "These people know exactly what I need - they're not
guessing."

Avoid generic agency language. Make it feel like a technical capability
overview, not a brochure.

SECTION 3: WHY CHOOSE US SECTION
File: components/home/ChooseUs.tsx
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Write content for EchoVerse 360's "Why Choose Us" section. There are 4 cards.
The section background text says "WHY CHOOSE ECHO VERSE" - keep that.

Pakistani audience: Brand owners and marketing heads who've worked with
local agencies that over-promise and under-deliver. They compare everything
to what they've seen internationally (globally-run brands like Unilever,
Pepsi, Coca-Cola operate here). EchoVerse is built by people who've actually
run those accounts.

For each card, write:
- Card Title (2-4 words, ALL CAPS)
- Card Body (2-3 sentences, 35-50 words total). Should feel like a specific
  differentiator, not a generic claim.

The 4 cards should tell a SEQUENTIAL STORY:
Card 1: We've built this from deep experience (credibility)
Card 2: We don't copy-paste - everything is custom (process)
Card 3: We use technology and data, not gut feeling (method)
Card 4: But ultimately, we care about your brand's story (humanity)

Journey moment: "This agency is different - they've actually done this at
scale before."

The tone per card should feel increasingly warm - starts authoritative,
ends human.

3. A "View All Projects" CTA micro-copy (5-8 words) - replace the generic

Rewrite 6 FAQs for EchoVerse 360's FAQ section. The section heading is
"SMALL QUESTIONS, BIG ANSWERS" - keep that.

Pakistani audience: Brand owners who are curious but cautious. They want
to know: Can you actually deliver? How do you measure success? What's the
minimum commitment? Do you work with small brands or only big ones?

Write 6 Q&A pairs. Requirements:
- Questions should sound like they're coming from a REAL Pakistani brand
  manager - not a generic FAQ list
- Answers should be 40-70 words: honest, specific, and confident without
  being defensive
- At least 2 questions should address ROI/measurement (because Pakistan
  market is skeptical of influencer marketing ROI)
- At least 1 question should address what makes EchoVerse different from
  the 50 other agencies offering the same service
- At least 1 question should address how to get started / minimum
  commitment
- Tone: like a senior partner answering a smart client's question - not
  sales-y, not corporate

Journey moment: "My last concern just got answered."

Rewrite the contact/footer section copy for EchoVerse 360. The current

Pakistani context: The call-to-action needs to feel low-pressure but
high-value. Pakistani decision-makers don't like being sold to. They want

5. Footer tagline (10-15 words) - a single line that sits in the bottom
   of the page, below the logo. Should summarize EchoVerse 360's entire
   reason for being, for a Pakistani market.
